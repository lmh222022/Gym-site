Understanding BMI

Underweight Below 18.5
Normal Weight 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight 25.0 - 29.9
Obesity Class I 30.0 - 34.9
Obesity Class II 35.0 - 39.9
Obesity Class III 40.0 and above

BMI is a screening tool but not a diagnostic of body fatness or health. Factors like muscle mass, age, sex, and ethnicity can influence the relationship between BMI and body fat.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a complete health assessment.
